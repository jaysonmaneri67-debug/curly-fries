📖 Plush Kaisen Lore

The Plush Kaisen universe began when plush characters awakened cursed energy.

Some gained power through training, while others awakened abilities through emotions.

The strongest fighters developed cursed techniques and domains that changed the balance of power.

Bonnie represents control and strategy, while Nightmare Bonnie represents unstoppable rage.
